01
AI Training · 18+ Months · Multi-platform
AI & Me: A Year Inside Production Pipelines
Andovar · LXT AI · Labelbox · Outlier · UberPrompt Engineer · Annotator · Multilingual QA
↓
The Problem

Modern AI systems fail not because of algorithms alone, but because of noisy data, weak labels, cultural blind spots, and poorly designed prompts. My role was to operate inside these fault lines — improve data fidelity before models reached production scale.

My Role
  • Image, video, audio, and text annotation
  • Prompt engineering and response evaluation
  • Multilingual QA — English, Hindi, Punjabi
  • Speech-to-text correction and voice dataset collection
  • Bias detection, hallucination spotting, output validation
Workstreams

Speech & Voice (Andovar) — Multilingual voice commands, transcript correction, accent robustness.

Computer Vision (LXT) — Bounding boxes, segmentation, skeleton maps for pose estimation.

LLM Evaluation (Outlier) — Prompts in 3 languages, response rating for accuracy and reasoning depth. Reduced hallucinations.

Classification & OCR (Uber) — Text domain classification, food image labeling, handwriting recognition.

Key Learnings
  • AI quality is a data problem before it's a model problem
  • Small annotation decisions compound at scale
  • Multilingual context is non-negotiable for global AI
  • Human judgment bridges raw models and usable systems

  • Retention is a stronger signal than raw impressions
  • Founder-led brands scale best with systems, not volume
